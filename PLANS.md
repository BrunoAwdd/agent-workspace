# Plans & Business Model

Agent Workspace separates the core coordination primitives from enterprise-level administration and governance. Our absolute priority is ensuring that the **Community Edition** remains fully capable, un-crippled, and production-ready for devs building multi-agent systems.

---

## 🟢 Community

_The open-source core. Free forever._

- **Runtime:** The coordination engine, embedded migrations, and database state management.
- **Primitives:** Tasks, Inbox, Locks, Handoffs, Dependencies, Events.
- **SDKs:** Python, TypeScript, and standard REST/MCP abstractions.
- **Deployment:** Docker `docker-compose.yml`, local binary CLI, and manual self-hosting guidelines.
- **Docs & Examples:** Unrestricted access to complex workflow patterns.

## 🏢 Enterprise _(Future)_

_Building confidence for complex organizational deployments._

- **Security:** Single Sign-On (SSO / SAML), advanced Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- **Governance:** Multi-tenant architecture, Audit controls (Audit Sinks), Policy Engine.
- **Data Controls:** Data retention/pruning controls, encrypted offsite backups.
- **Admin Tools:** Dedicated graphical dashboards for fleet management.
- **SLA:** Enterprise-grade support guarantees.

## 🤝 Services _(Future)_

_For teams accelerating their AI adoption._

- **Consulting:** Architecture design tailored to specific company topologies.
- **Integration:** Bespoke webhook setups, pipeline deployments, and custom primitive abstractions.

## ☁️ Hosted _(Future)_

_Cloud Managed._

- True zero-infrastructure cloud offering. Deploy your agents locally, point them to our cloud runtime natively.
